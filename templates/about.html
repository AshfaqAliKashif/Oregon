Enhance Your Commute, Transform Your Travel

With a rich legacy spanning more than three decades, Love Tires and Wheels has solidified its position as a premier provider of high-caliber automotive accessories. Our dedicated team is unwaveringly committed to elevating your vehicle through an array of services, encompassing precise wheel and tire installations, expert application of lift or lowering kits, and the installation of cutting-edge performance exhaust systems. Our unwavering focus on unparalleled craftsmanship, safety, and customer satisfaction sets us apart, all while maintaining a commitment to competitive pricing.

Our comprehensive suite of services caters to the discerning needs of customers in Medford, Oregon, and the surrounding areas. At Love Tires and Wheels, we take pride in offering bilingual assistance in both English and Spanish, ensuring seamless communication and understanding. Choose Love Tires and Wheels as your preferred destination for all your vehicle enhancement needs, where a perfect blend of experience, expertise, and excellence awaits you.
